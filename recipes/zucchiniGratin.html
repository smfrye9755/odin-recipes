Zuchinni Gratin

A simple vegetable gratin.

Ingredients

  • 2+ Tbsp. Olive oil
  • 4-8 Zuchinni
  • 1/2 cup sliced Onion
  • 1-2 Tbsp minced Garlic
  • 2-4 tsp finely chopped rosemary
  • 2-4 Tbsp finely chopped parseley
  • 6+ Tbsp parmessan

Instrucitons

  1. Add oil to a large skillet set on medium-high heat and stir fry the onion and zuchinni until slightly browned. Do so in batches if needed.
  2. Mix in garlic, rosemary, and parseley. Stir fry until fragrant
  3. Meanwhile, preheat broiler for 5 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish sitr at least 2 Tbsp of parmessan with the zuchinni, spread the remaining cheese on top and broil until cheese is browned.
