Pad Thai Inspired Fried Noodles

Tofu and vegetables pan fried in green curry to create a crispy dish in a curry glaze.

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 4 tsp. Soy Sauce
  • 5 tsp. brown sugar
  • 2 tsp. rice vinegar
  • 4 tsp. crunchy peanutbutter (no added sugar)

Noodles

  • Noodles (egg or ramen)
  • Butter
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1-2 eggs
  • Partially cooked vegetables and/or protein, chopped to small pieces.

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles and mix the sauce
  2. In a small pan on medium-high, melth the butter then add garlic. Cook until fragrant.
  3. Stir fry noodles with egg.
  4. Mix in the vegetables and protein, fry until cooked.
  5. Mix in the sauce.
