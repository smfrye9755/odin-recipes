Green Curry Glazed Tofu

Tofu and vegetables pan fried in green curry to create a crispy dish in a curry glaze.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg tofu
  • ~4 Tbsp. green curry paste
  • 1 (14 oz) can coconut milk
  • 1/2 tsp. light brown sugar
  • 2+ cups vegetables such as carrots, snap peas, etc.
  • High heat cooking oil (such as rapeseed or avocado)
  • Juice from 1 lime
  • Rice, noodles, or other grains.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables, chopping all to a similar size.
  2. Cut tofu into 12 squares (1/2 lengthwise, 1/6 crosswise). Pat dry on a cutting board with paper towels.
  3. Mix curry paste, coconut milk and sugar with a whisk.
  4. Season skillet with oil, then arrange tofu over skillet. Coat tofu in roughly 2 Tbsp curry.
  5. Fry over medium-high heat for ~4 minutes, then flip once underside is golden and curry has evaporated.
  6. Add ~2 Tbsp oilwhile flipping tofu. Cook until bottom is browned (~4 minutes), then flip back and cook for 1 more minute before setting tofu aside on a plate.
  7. Cook vegetables, undstutbed until charred underneath.
  8. Add curry sauce and boil. Let liquid thicken to a glaze.
  9. Remove from heat. Gently stir in lime juice and tofu. Serve with rice, noodles, or other grain.
